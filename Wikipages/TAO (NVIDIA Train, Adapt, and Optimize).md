# Requirements

### Hardware Requirements (Recommended)

    32 GB system RAM

    32 GB of GPU RAM

    8 core CPU

    1 NVIDIA GPU

    100 GB of SSD space

### Hardware Requirements (REQUIRED)

     - TAO Toolkit is supported on **A100**, **V100** and **RTX 30x0 GPUs**.
